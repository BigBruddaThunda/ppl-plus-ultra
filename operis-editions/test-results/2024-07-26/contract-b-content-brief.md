## Word of the Day
**Protocol**
From Greek *protokollon* (first glue sheet attached to a manuscript roll), later Latin and French forms shifted meaning toward official procedural form. In modern usage, protocol is a formal set of rules enabling reliable interaction across people and systems. The date's strongest material—postal systems, legal accessibility standards, and Olympic execution models—converges on protocol as structure that makes coordination possible. For this edition, the word cues disciplined format rather than stylistic rigidity.
