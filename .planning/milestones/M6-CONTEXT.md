## Type-Specific Correction Targets

The ⚖ Order targets different weakness profiles by Type:

| Type | Correction Target |
|------|-----------------|
| 🛒 Push | Triceps, side delts, front delts (often overpowered by chest) |
| 🪡 Pull | Biceps, rear delts, forearms, grip (often underdeveloped vs. lat dominance) |
| 🍗 Legs | Calves, adductors, tibialis (often neglected in squat-dominant programs) |
| ➕ Plus | Rotational stability, anti-rotation (core gaps) |
| ➖ Ultra | Energy system gaps, aerobic base (often missing in strength-focused athletes) |

## Key Block: 🏗 Reformance

🏗 Reformance (corrective construction) is the defining block of ⚖ Balance. It appears prominently here — more so than in any other Order. It contains prehab, postural correction, and targeted accessory compound work addressing the specific gap identified by the Type.

🧈 Bread & Butter in ⚖ context = targeted accessory compounds addressing the specific gap, not the main compound lift.
