   - BLOCKS: 22 entries keyed by position 1-22 (♨️, 🎯, 🔢, 🧈, 🫀, ▶️, 🎼, ♟️, 🪜, 🌎, 🎱, 🌋, 🪞, 🗿, 🛠, 🧩, 🪫, 🏖, 🏗, 🧬, 🚂, 🔠)

   - OPERATOR_BY_EMOJI, BLOCK_BY_EMOJI

   - Orders at 1-7, Axes at 8-13, Types at 14-18, Colors at 19-26

   - TOTAL_ZIPS = 1680
