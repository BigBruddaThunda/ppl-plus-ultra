# Seal Row (Barbell)

## What It Is

Seal Row (Barbell) is a horizontal pull movement that primarily trains latissimus_dorsi, rhomboids, trapezius_middle. It sits in the Pull type of the SCL system and is classified as a compound movement. 

## Setup

- Chest to the pad or overhand grip engaged before initiating the pull.
- Shoulders down and back before the elbow bends.
- Core braced — the torso does not rotate.
- Dead stop or controlled stretch at the bottom on each rep.

## Execution

- Lead with the elbow — not the hand.
- Squeeze the shoulder blade at the top of every rep.
- Lower the weight under control — the eccentric is half the stimulus.
- No torso rocking for momentum.
- Full stretch at the bottom: let the shoulder blade move forward.

## Common Faults

- **Pulling with the bicep instead of the back:** Think: elbow to hip, not hand to chest. The lat drives, the bicep assists.
- **Torso rocking for momentum:** The torso position is fixed. All movement comes from the shoulder blade and arm.
- **Shrugging the shoulder at the top:** Shoulder stays depressed. The blade retracts, not elevates.

## What It Trains

**Primary:** latissimus_dorsi, rhomboids, trapezius_middle
**Secondary:** biceps_brachii, rear_deltoid, teres_major
**Joint actions:** shoulder_horizontal_abduction, elbow_flexion, scapular_retraction

## PPL± Context

**In Foundation (🐂):** Row mechanics: shoulders set first, pull to the lower rib, squeeze the shoulder blade at the top. Foundation load is pattern acquisition — the groove before the grind.

**In Strength (⛽):** Heavy rows are a grip and lat event. 75–85% loads the lats, rhomboids, and rear delts heavily. The row is not a bicep exercise at this intensity. Brace the core. The torso does not rotate.

**In Hypertrophy (🦋):** Full stretch at the dead hang, full retraction at the top. The back gets its stimulus from range, not just load. Slow eccentric on cable and dumbbell rows amplifies this.

**In Restoration (🖼):** Face pulls, band rows, or TRX rows at low angle. The posterior chain of the shoulder needs circulation after heavy pressing and pulling. This is maintenance, not development.

**Color modifiers:**
- Technical (🟣): Pause at full contraction. Hold the blade together for 1 second at the top of every rep. The technical lens exposes retraction quality.
- Intense (🔴): Row superset with face pull or rear delt fly. Posterior shoulder volume accumulated across angles.
- Mindful (⚪): 4-second eccentric to full dead-hang or full cable stretch. Exhale on the pull. Feel the scapula move before the arm.
