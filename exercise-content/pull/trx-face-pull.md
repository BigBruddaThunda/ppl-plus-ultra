# TRX Face Pull

## What It Is

TRX Face Pull is a horizontal pull movement that primarily trains core, transversus_abdominis, rectus_abdominis. It sits in the Pull type of the SCL system and is classified as an isolation movement. 

## Setup

- Find neutral spine — not flat back, not excessive arch.
- 360-degree brace: belly out, back wide, pelvic floor engaged.
- Shoulders packed and away from the ears.
- Breath: in through the nose, brace, move. Exhale on the effort or hold.

## Execution

- Build pressure before moving — brace first, move second.
- Maintain neutral spine throughout — no spinal flexion/extension under load.
- Breathe: in through the nose, brace, move. Exhale on the effort.
- If position is lost, reset before continuing.
- Quality over time: a 20-second perfect plank beats a 60-second sagging one.

## Common Faults

- **Holding breath instead of bracing:** Brace first, breathe into the brace. They are separate actions. Holding breath is a Valsalva choice for max effort, not the default.
- **Hips hiking in plank or dead bug:** Find neutral pelvis first. The low back should have a slight natural curve, not flatten completely.
- **Arms/legs moving before core is set:** Setup order: position, brace, breathe, move. Never the reverse.

## What It Trains

**Primary:** core, transversus_abdominis, rectus_abdominis
**Secondary:** obliques, erector_spinae, hip_flexors
**Joint actions:** spinal_flexion, spinal_stabilization, intra_abdominal_pressure

## PPL± Context

**In Foundation (🐂):** Row mechanics: shoulders set first, pull to the lower rib, squeeze the shoulder blade at the top. Foundation load is pattern acquisition — the groove before the grind.

**In Strength (⛽):** Heavy rows are a grip and lat event. 75–85% loads the lats, rhomboids, and rear delts heavily. The row is not a bicep exercise at this intensity. Brace the core. The torso does not rotate.

**In Hypertrophy (🦋):** Full stretch at the dead hang, full retraction at the top. The back gets its stimulus from range, not just load. Slow eccentric on cable and dumbbell rows amplifies this.

**In Restoration (🖼):** Face pulls, band rows, or TRX rows at low angle. The posterior chain of the shoulder needs circulation after heavy pressing and pulling. This is maintenance, not development.

**Color modifiers:**
- Technical (🟣): Pause at full contraction. Hold the blade together for 1 second at the top of every rep. The technical lens exposes retraction quality.
- Intense (🔴): Row superset with face pull or rear delt fly. Posterior shoulder volume accumulated across angles.
- Mindful (⚪): 4-second eccentric to full dead-hang or full cable stretch. Exhale on the pull. Feel the scapula move before the arm.
