# 🛒 Floor Press — Chest, Slow Eccentric 🛒

4-second eccentric barbell strength | Chest, front delts, triceps | 70–80 min

CODE: ⛽🏛🛒⚪

> "The weight goes down on your schedule, not gravity's."

═══════════════════════════════════════

## 1. ♨️ WARM-UP

📍 pono — arrive slowly

5 deep breaths. Then light movement — shoulder circles, arm swings.

- 10 Shoulder Dislocations (4 sec per rep)
- 12 🛒 Barbell Bench Press (empty bar, 4 sec down)
- 10 🛒 Barbell Overhead Press (empty bar, 3 sec down)

Breathing for the session: inhale on the way down, exhale on the press. Every rep.

═══════════════════════════════════════

## 2. ▶️ PRIMER

📍 pono — ramp up under tempo

4-second eccentric applies to all ramp sets.

Bench Press:
- Set 1: 52% × 5 (4 sec down, pause 1 sec)
- Set 2: 63% × 4 (same — feel the pec loaded in the stretch)
- Set 3: 72% × 2 (near working weight)

Rest: 2 min between sets.

═══════════════════════════════════════

## 3. 🧈 BREAD & BUTTER

📍 pono — heavy slow bench, one movement

Tempo: 4-1-2. Four seconds down, one second pause, two seconds up.
Rest: 3 min between sets.

**🛒 Barbell Floor Press**

| Set | Load | Reps |
|-----|------|------|
| 1 | 78% | 4 |
| 2 | 80% | 4 |
| 3 | 80% | 4 |
| 4 | 78% | 4 |
| 5 | 78% | 4 |

Bar to lower sternum. Inhale at the top, hold through the descent, exhale on the press.

The eccentric is harder than it looks at 80%. Four reps is correct. Do not chase five.

═══════════════════════════════════════

## 4. 🪫 RELEASE

📍 pono — parasympathetic downregulation, tension down

Don't rush this. The release is half the session in ⚪.

- Seated breathing: 2 min (4 count in, 4 hold, 8 out)
- Doorway chest stretch: 90 sec each side
- Child's pose (arms extended): 90 sec
- Cross-body shoulder stretch: 45 sec each side

Leave quieter than you arrived.
