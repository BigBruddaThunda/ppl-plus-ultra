# Deck 12 Identity — ⛽🐬 Strength Partner

## 1) Deck Philosophy
Deck 12 applies ⛽ Strength loading law (75–85%, 4–6 reps, 3–4 min rest, high CNS) to the 🐬 Partner axis so two athletes share one heavy structure: one lifter works while one partner spots, cues, paces, and audits execution.

The partner is structural in every room: alternating heavy sets, synchronized compound patterns, competitive loading ladders, and assisted negatives when quality would otherwise drop. Preparatory colors run 🧲 capio; expressive colors run 🦉 logos.

## 3) Color Differentiation Logic
- ⚫ Teaching — coached partner drills with verbal cueing, spotted setup, and load management checks each set.
- 🟢 Bodyweight — bodyweight partner resistance, assisted reps, and positional holds without gym dependency.
- 🔵 Structured — prescribed alternating heavy sets with fixed rest and written partner handoff rhythm.
- 🟣 Technical — spotted heavy singles/doubles with form feedback and strict quality thresholds.
- 🔴 Intense — competitive loading waves, partner-forced reps, and short tactical transitions.
- 🟠 Circuit — you-go-I-go station rotation with heavy non-barbell implements and loop-safe sequencing.
- 🟡 Fun — exploratory partner challenges, load games, and rotating task constraints inside strength law.
- ⚪ Mindful — slow heavy negatives with partner assist, mindful spotting, and breath-paced resets.
