# Deck 11 Identity — ⛽⌛ Strength Time

## 1) Deck Philosophy
Deck 11 is heavy strength work governed by the clock. The loading law stays ⛽ Strength (75–85%, 4–6 reps, 3–4 min rest, high CNS demand), but the axis lens is ⌛ Time, so every session organizes those heavy efforts through explicit timing structures.

The clock is not a conditioning gimmick in this deck. It is a pacing mechanism for high-quality force output: EMOM heavy singles, density waves of compound lifts, timed maximal attempts, and time-capped strength circuits that keep rep quality inside fixed windows.

Operator split is fixed by polarity: preparatory colors use 🐋 duco (conduct the session rhythm), expressive colors use ✒️ grapho (prescribe and record exact timed output).

## 3) Color Differentiation Logic
- ⚫ Teaching — timed teaching blocks with extended coaching and clear timer callouts.
- 🟢 Bodyweight — bodyweight EMOM and density formats that keep strength intent without barbells.
- 🔵 Structured — prescribed EMOM/interval templates with exact work-rest intervals and log targets.
- 🟣 Technical — timed singles and doubles with quality gates, pauses, and strict rep standards.
- 🔴 Intense — density blocks and heavy AMRAP-style windows with reduced rest and high output.
- 🟠 Circuit — timed station rotation using non-barbell tools with loop logic and tissue alternation.
- 🟡 Fun — exploratory time-based formats (ladders, odd intervals, mixed modalities) within strength constraints.
- ⚪ Mindful — slow-tempo heavy work with extended rest windows and breath-paced execution.
