## 3) Color Differentiation Logic

- **âš« Teaching:** Deck 08 teaching sessions slow unilateral setup down so stance, rib position, and contralateral brace are coached before heavy loading. Exercise choice favors controllable patterns where error correction is visible rep to rep.
- **ğŸŸ¢ Bodyweight:** Bodyweight functional strength uses unilateral calisthenics progressions (pistol, archer push-up, crawls, single-leg bridges) to prove gym strength transfers without machines or racks. Selection excludes fixed-path work and prioritizes positional ownership.
- **ğŸ”µ Structured:** Structured cards use the most repeatable unilateral lifts and interval formats so the logbook drives progression. Exercise selection prioritizes clean comparability week to week.
- **ğŸŸ£ Technical:** Technical cards narrow exercise count and choose precision-biased unilateral movements (landmine, get-up, half-kneeling cable, cadence control). Rest is longer and misses are technical, not metabolic.
- **ğŸ”´ Intense:** Intense cards choose unilateral exercises that can safely absorb fatigue in supersets and denser blocks. Movements needing maximal coordination under low fatigue are filtered out.
- **ğŸŸ  Circuit:** Circuit cards rotate stations across tissues and movement patterns with no barbell dependency. Exercise selection is loop-safe and alternates stress to keep quality in timed rotations.
- **ğŸŸ¡ Fun:** Fun cards expand unilateral vocabulary with less common patterns and grip/plane variation while still respecting Strength order ceilings. The goal is exposure without randomness.
- **âšª Mindful:** Mindful cards enforce 4-second eccentrics, pauses, and breath pacing on unilateral patterns that improve proprioception and stability. Exercise choice favors controllable tension over maximal output.
