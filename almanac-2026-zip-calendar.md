The daily zip code for every day of 2026, derived from the three-gear rotation engine.

## How to Read This Calendar

Every day has three fixed dials and one user-choice dial:

```
ZIP = [ORDER] [AXIS] [TYPE] [COLOR]
       ↓        ↓      ↓      ↓
    Weekday   Month  Rolling  Yours
```

- **ORDER** — fixed to the day of the week (never changes)
- **AXIS** — fixed to the month (shifts 12 times per year)
- **TYPE** — rolls forward from Jan 1 on a 5-day cycle, never resets for the week
- **COLOR** — your choice: ⚫🟢🔵🟣🔴🟠🟡⚪

To read any cell: the row header gives ORDER, the column position gives the date, the cell emoji gives TYPE, and the month section header gives AXIS.

**Full zip for any day:** ROW-EMOJI + MONTH-AXIS-EMOJI + CELL-EMOJI + COLOR-CHOICE
